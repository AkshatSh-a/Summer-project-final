AI in Social Science for Sustainability and Consumer Rights

AI aids policymakers in developing sustainable and consumer-friendly policies by analyzing data, predicting policy impacts, and improving public engagement. This leads to informed decision-making that balances environmental goals with consumer protection.

AI's Role in Policy and Governance

AI supports governance through:

  • Policy impact analysis using past data for better decisions.
  • Encouraging sustainable business practices while protecting consumers.
  • Ensuring compliance with sustainability regulations.
  • Enhancing public engagement to reflect community needs in policies.
  • Efficient resource allocation to promote sustainability.
  • Analyzing economic impacts to balance growth with environmental objectives.

AI's data-driven insights and public interaction tools are key to creating a more sustainable and consumer-friendly society.
